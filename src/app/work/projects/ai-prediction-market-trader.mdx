## Overview

The AI Prediction Market Trader is an autonomous agent designed to participate in prediction markets on the Gnosis blockchain. It uses machine learning models to analyze market data, predict outcomes, and execute trades automatically, demonstrating the potential of AI agents in decentralized finance.

## Key Features

- **Market Analysis**: Continuously monitors prediction markets for trading opportunities
- **ML-Powered Predictions**: Uses ensemble models to forecast market outcomes
- **Risk Management**: Implements sophisticated position sizing and stop-loss strategies
- **Autonomous Execution**: Executes trades automatically based on confidence thresholds
- **Performance Tracking**: Comprehensive analytics and reporting on trading performance
- **Blockchain Integration**: Seamless interaction with Gnosis smart contracts

## Technologies Used

- **Python**: Core implementation language
- **Scikit-learn**: Machine learning models for prediction
- **Web3.py**: Blockchain interaction
- **Gnosis Protocol**: Prediction market infrastructure
- **PostgreSQL**: Trade history and analytics storage
- **Docker**: Containerized deployment

## Trading Strategy

The agent employs a multi-layered approach:

1. **Data Collection**: Aggregates data from prediction markets, news sources, and social sentiment
2. **Feature Engineering**: Extracts relevant features including market momentum, volume patterns, and sentiment scores
3. **Ensemble Prediction**: Combines multiple ML models (Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, Neural Networks) for robust predictions
4. **Confidence Scoring**: Only trades when prediction confidence exceeds threshold
5. **Position Management**: Dynamic position sizing based on confidence and risk tolerance
6. **Execution**: Optimized trade execution to minimize slippage

## Challenges and Learnings

Prediction markets are inherently uncertain and require careful risk management. The key challenge was building models that could distinguish between genuine edge and noise. This required extensive backtesting, careful feature selection, and robust validation frameworks. Additionally, blockchain transaction costs and latency required optimizing trade execution strategies.

## Technical Innovations

- **Adaptive Confidence Thresholds**: Dynamically adjusts trading thresholds based on market conditions
- **Multi-Market Arbitrage**: Identifies and exploits price discrepancies across related markets
- **Sentiment Integration**: Incorporates social media sentiment analysis into predictions
- **Gas Optimization**: Minimizes transaction costs through batch processing and timing optimization
